Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

    • Ingredients:

    • 3 eggs
    • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
    • 1/4 cup milk
    • 1/4 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 1 tbsp butter or oil for cooking

    Instructions:

    1. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
    2. Heat butter or oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
    3. Add chopped spinach and cook until wilted, about 2 minutes.
    4. Pour the egg mixture over the spinach, tilting the pan to spread evenly.
    5. Cook until the edges start to set, about 2-3 minutes.
    6. Sprinkle shredded cheese over one half of the omelette.
    7. Using a spatula, fold the other half over the cheese.
    8. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until the cheese melts and the omelette is set.
    9. Slide onto a plate and serve hot.
